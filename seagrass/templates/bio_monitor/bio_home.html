About Us

Advanced bio-monitoring for tracking aquatic ecosystem health and stress indicators.

The bio-monitor is an advanced system designed to continuously track the physiological responses of aquatic organisms, especially seagrass. It integrates real-time sensors to measure parameters like dissolved oxygen, pH, temperature, and chlorophyll concentration. This system provides early detection of stress conditions caused by environmental fluctuations or pollutants. By analyzing photosynthetic efficiency and growth indicators, it ensures optimal cultivation conditions. It supports long-term monitoring and generates valuable data for ecological research and restoration. The device is modular and can be deployed in both lab-scale and field environments. It helps researchers understand tolerance thresholds and adaptive behaviors of marine plants. Data collected can be used to predict ecosystem shifts due to climate or anthropogenic factors. It enables proactive intervention in case of stress or imbalance in the system. Overall, the bio-monitor plays a key role in sustainable aquatic farming and marine conservation efforts.
