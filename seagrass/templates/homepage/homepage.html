Modular Marine Farming Unit

The Modular Marine Farming Unit is a scalable solution designed for the efficient cultivation of seagrass. It supports ecosystem restoration, carbon capture, and marine biodiversity through sustainable and adaptable growth environments. The system features modular components that can be customized based on location, water conditions, and species requirements. Its design allows easy deployment in coastal areas, enabling both small-scale pilot studies and large-scale habitat restoration projects. By mimicking natural conditions and maintaining optimal light, nutrients, and sediment stability, the unit ensures healthy seagrass propagation. This innovative approach not only aids in marine conservation but also contributes to climate resilience and sustainable coastal development.
