About Us

Calculate surface, volume, density, weight for manufacturing

AquaForge is responsible for calculating the amount of material needed to manufacture the underwater tool. It determines the surface area of the box, then multiplies it by material thickness to get the volume. Using the material's density, it calculates the total weight required for production. This ensures efficient and accurate material usage.
